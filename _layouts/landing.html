About the Open Exchange

  • What is the Open Exchange?

    The Open Exchange, which you can identify as the IODC Unconference, is a space where we can exchange experiences and learn from each other in a safe, horizontal manner

  • Why an Unconference?

    Conferences are a great place for networking and learning different topics, but they are organised in a way that is less participatory. We want to complement the work being done during IODC
