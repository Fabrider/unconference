What is the Open Data and Social Change Fest?

Previously known as the IODC Unconference, the Fest will be a space where we can exchange experiences and learn from each other in a safe, horizontal space

Ok, so what's an Unconference then?

An unconference is an open event that allows its members to propose their own topics for discussion in an open and horizontal way

And why do an Unconference?

Conferences are a great place for networking, learning different topics and sharing achievements (and sometimes even failures), but in their nature, they are organised in a way that is less participatory and more broadcast than an exchange of information.

Can I attend?

If you will attend IODC, you can definitely come to the Open Data and Social Impact Fest! We can't provide travel support, so you need to take that into account before signing up
